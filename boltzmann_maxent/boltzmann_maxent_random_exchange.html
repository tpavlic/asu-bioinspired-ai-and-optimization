Boltzmann Distribution via Random Exchange
N agents repeatedly trade random amounts of wealth. Total wealth W is strictly conserved. The maximum-entropy equilibrium for any conserved non-negative quantity is P(w) = (1/μ) e−w/μ — regardless of initial conditions.
